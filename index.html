About Us

Vignan's Institute Of Information Technology is one of the engineering institutions run by the Vignan group of Guntur. It was established in 2002 to offer undergraduate (BTech) (college code:L3) courses in engineering and technology. It is situated in Duvvada, a suburban region of Visakhapatnam, India.

Activities and Facitilies

Every Year the annual fest "Vignanosthav" is Organized by the college and the students together.

Administration is very friendly with all the students and motivate students to enhance their skills.

Integrated Labs with High Speed Internet and High-end Systems help student learn and apply the theory.
